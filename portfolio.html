TRANCAO!

TRANCAO! is a Roblox game currently in development that brings the classic Dominican dominoes experience into a fun and interactive digital world. The game is set in a lively neighborhood inspired by the capital of the Dominican Republic, complete with authentic details that capture the culture and atmosphere of the area. Players will be able to sit down at the dominoes table, compete with friends, and enjoy the social, competitive spirit of this beloved game, all while exploring a map that feels like home.
